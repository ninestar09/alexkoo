Creative Entrepreneurial Leader

An award-winning 3D artist /Art director, XR contents developer and Entrepreneurial with 15+ years of creative professional experience with focus on Creative Concept/identity development, Interactivity and communication for Rich Media.

Incredible experiences happen at the intersection of narrative and cutting edge technology. Throughout my 15 year career from interactive installation art to global commercial production, I have focused on closing the gap between complex technical systems and the human stories they are meant to tell. Whether working with real time 3D, generative AI, or spatial interaction, my goal is to ensure the technology never overshadows the message.

As a Senior 3D Developer at CBS News, I specialize in the architecture of immersion. From virtual production pipelines to massive LED volumes, I design environments where physical and digital storytelling become indistinguishable. My focus is simple: maintaining the highest level of visual excellence while ensuring technology scales to meet the moment.

As an adjunct professor at NYU, I am currently researching the friction between AI and production. This led to the creation of RYZA, an AI-native platform that turns generative potential into production ready reality. I believe the future is “phygital”, a world where brand stories aren't contained by walls, but expanded by them.

Whether I am teaching or leading a creative team, I am dedicated to representation and accessibility. As we build the next generation of experiential media, my mission is to open doors and lower barriers for creators everywhere, ensuring the future of 3D and AI remains authentic and inclusive.
