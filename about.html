My principles

Design Philosophy

Three principles guide every project I take on — from broadcast LED volumes to AI-native creative tools.

01

Narrative First

Technology is a medium, not the message. Every design decision starts with the story — the human experience at the center of the work. If the technology draws more attention than the narrative, something has gone wrong.

02

Phygital by Design

The most resonant experiences dissolve the boundary between physical and digital. I design for that seam — building immersive environments where the two worlds don't compete, but amplify each other.

03

Accessible Excellence

World-class creative work should be democratized. I build pipelines, platforms, and educational frameworks that lower barriers without compromising ambition — because the future of 3D and AI must be inclusive to be authentic.
